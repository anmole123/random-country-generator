Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the Random Country Generator

Countries are selected randomly from a comprehensive list of all recognized sovereign states. When online, we fetch the latest data from the REST Countries API. If you're offline or the API is unavailable, we use our embedded dataset with detailed information for each country.

Yes! Our advanced features include region and language filters. You can specify which regions (like Europe, Asia, Africa) or languages you're interested in, and the generator will only select countries that match your criteria. These options are available in the "Advanced Features" section.

Your favorite countries are stored locally in your browser's storage. This means we don't have access to your favorites, and they remain private to you. If you clear your browser data, your favorites will be lost, so we recommend exporting them if you want to keep a permanent record.

The Random Country Generator works completely offline! We include a comprehensive dataset with information for all countries directly in the application. While offline, you won't get live flag images from the API, but all other country details will be available from our embedded dataset.

After generating a country, you'll see a "Share" button. Clicking this will give you options to copy a summary of the country information to your clipboard or use your device's native sharing capabilities to send it via email, social media, or messaging apps. The summary includes key facts about the country in a formatted text.
